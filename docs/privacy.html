Privacy Policy

Conesoft Tools · Last updated: June 25, 2026

Conesoft Tools ("the app", "we", "us") is a desktop application for converting and editing files (images, video, audio, PDFs and more). This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used. Our core principle is local-first processing: your files are converted on your own device and are never uploaded to us or to any third party.

1. Your files stay on your device

All file conversion and editing (images, video, audio, PDF, SVG, screenshots, favicons, etc.) runs entirely on your computer. The contents of the files you convert are never transmitted to our servers or to any third party. We do not see, store, or have access to the files you process.

2. Information we collect

We only collect personal information if you choose to create an account or purchase a paid plan:

  • Account information — if you sign up, we store your email address and authentication credentials. Authentication is handled by Supabase.
  • Usage metering — to enforce free-trial and plan limits, we store counts of how many conversions you have performed and your remaining quota ("tokens"). This is tied to your account, not to the content of your files.
  • App settings — if you are signed in, your preferences (default output formats, image quality, output folder) are synced to your account so they are available across devices.
  • Payment information — purchases are processed by Paddle, our payment provider and merchant of record. We never receive or store your full card details. We store only the identifiers Paddle provides (such as a customer ID and subscription ID) so we can manage your plan and renewals.

3. How we use this information

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To enforce free-trial limits and the entitlements of your paid plan.
  • To sync your settings across devices.
  • To process payments, renewals, and cancellations.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising. The app contains no third-party advertising or analytics trackers.

4. Service providers

We rely on a small number of trusted providers to operate the account and payment features:

  • Supabase — account authentication and database storage (email, plan, usage counts, settings).
  • Paddle — payment processing and subscription management.

These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies, linked above.

5. Data retention

We retain account information for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account or request deletion, your associated personal data is removed from our systems, except where we are required to retain transaction records for legal or accounting purposes.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at the address below. Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA.

7. Children

Conesoft Tools is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
